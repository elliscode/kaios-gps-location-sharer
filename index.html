Help

How do I share my location?

Simply press the "Share Location Data" button in the app. Then, press the "SMS Live Location Updates" button to share the link with your unique identifier.

Do I need to make an account?

No, when you first open the app, it generates a unique identifier which is used for location sharing. You can change this unique identifier at any time by pressing "Generate New ID" in the "Options" menu.

Who can see my location?

Anyone with the link can see your location data.

Where is my location data sent?

Your location data is securely sent to our database hosted in the AWS cloud.

How often is my location updated?

You can customize how often the data is sent to our servers, anywhere between once every 5 seconds, to once every 30 seconds.

How long can I share my location?

You can customize how long the app shares your location before turning itself off, anywhere from 15 minutes to 120 minutes.

How long is my location data kept after I stop sharing?

Your most recently shared location is kept in our database for 1 hour, after that it is automatically deleted from our database.

What other data do you collect?

We store the IP address the request came from, the random identifier which sent the request, and some other headers provided by AWS API Gateway. We do not store the location data in our logs. Our logs are kept for 2 years before being deleted.

Do you sell my data?

We do not sell your data, nor will we ever sell your data. This is a hobby project and we will simply shut down the service if it starts costing too much money.

What if the person I'm sharing with has a different kind of phone?

Our location viewer page opens in a web browser, and works on both KaiOS devices as well as traditional smartphones, such as those running iOS or Android.
